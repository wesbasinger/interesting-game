Getting Started

Beginning Balance

You begin the game with $10,000. The goal is to accrue as much money as possible. There is no way to add additional cash, you must make your fortune from interest and the like.

Time

Every hour counts for one year. You do not need to stay logged in for your accounts to mature. You can log out and they will continue to grow interest and mature until you log back in. The basic flow should be to invest all your free cash. Cash earns no interest.

Different Account Types

Savings Account

This is a very safe option. There is no risk of losing your investment and it does pay an interest rate. You can liquidate this type of account at any time.

Mutual Fund

This account does not pay interest. Instead, your returns are based on the NASDAQ stock index. You must make a fairly large investment to purchase one share, but there is more potential for returns. The idea is to buy low and sell high. However, there is some risk associated with this type of account.

Money Market

This account pays higher interest than a savings account, but there is a slight possibility that the account could crash and you would lose your investment. There is a minimum investment in this type of account.

Government Bond

This investment pays a nice interest rate, but requires that you leave the money in the account for some predecided duration of time. You cannot liquidate the account until the fund matures. There is also some risk associated with this type of account.

Compound Deposit

This is a lot like a goverment bond, except with a much safer interest rate. You must leave the money in the fund until it matures. There is no chance of losing money in this investment.

Horse Racing

To add an element of fun, I have included a group of illicit investments. The returns are ridiculously high, but don't expect to get your money back a majority of the time! There's a price to pay for crime :-)
